OUR IDEAL SCHOOL

Our ideal school starts at 9 o'clock and finishes at 4 p.m.

For five hours students partecipate at lessons and for last two hours they can do their homework.

They are classrooms for each subject and , when an hour finishes, students must change room within 10 minutes.

Sometimes, when the weather is sunny, lessons can be done outside, indeed school offers a spacious garden which is cared every day

In every classrooms, there are personal computers (for each desk) and personal lockers.

Moreover, every week, school invite university students to answer questions.

In our ideal school there are many sports fields (for each sport) and every month there are some tornauments between sections.

To conclude we must to talk about means of trasport, indeed there are some school buses to avoid confusion when students leaving school.
